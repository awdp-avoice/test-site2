Team Support

Service Introduction

Our volunteer team will provide you with free support services for anti-domestic violence and other gender-based violence. Our services include legal support, emotional support, language support, shelter applications, relief fund applications, employment assistance, and comprehensive support.

We provide both online and offline support. We offer offline support in Belgium, while in other countries and regions, you can choose to receive our online support services via WeChat, WhatsApp group chat, text, voice, and phone.

Targeted Service Recipients

Our services are targeted towards individuals who meet the following three criteria:

  • Your request is related to “gender-based violence,” such as domestic violence, sexual assault, sexual harassment, etc.

  • You are a Chinese (ethnic) woman, regardless of whether your current nationality belongs to China or not;

  • You are currently residing outside mainland China.

Service Fees

All services provided by this project are free of charge, and we will not charge you in any way. We will also not charge any intermediate fees when providing third-party referral services for you.
